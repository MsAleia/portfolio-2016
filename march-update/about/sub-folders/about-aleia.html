Hi again, I'm Aleia

A self-taught frontend developer + designer with a passion for mobile-first responsive design, minimalism, and user experience.

I attended the University of Miami where I received degrees in Creative Writing and International Studies. I intended to develop a career in academia but I pivoted after following my masters. I spent a few months as a freelance writer before taking a job where I serve customers everyday.

And then I found web development...

I revisted my graphic design design skills and translated them to UX and UI skills. I chose a text editor and then got to work finding my niche in web development. My sweet spot starts around UX and ends around the development of a responsive layout + JavaScript functionality. I share my journey online, mostly on Twitter, and have found an amazing tech community. I'm missing that in real life and can't wait to find a team as I continue to realize my dev dreams.

And the rest is future.

You can find me... reading books about design and development. trying new restaurants. writing things about design, development, and getting started in the tech industry. finding tea shops. building mastermind groups for individuals looking to make things happen. reggae + dancehall musics. finding size 6 wide calf boots. living in walkable cities.
