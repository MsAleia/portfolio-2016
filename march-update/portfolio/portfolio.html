Responsive Design
Web Design, Frontend, Bootstrap

Loud Vibes is a small reggae and dancehall concert site designed to share upcoming concerts, reviews, and music news-while keeping gossip to a minimum.

I started by creating user personas for Loud Vibes target users. The target users are true fans of reggae and dancehall and use it as their outlet from their very hectic lives. This led me to pay more attention to the mobile experience.

This is the first project I created a style tile for instead of a mood board and I am really excited about how functional they are when it is time to code. All of my design elements are available here.
