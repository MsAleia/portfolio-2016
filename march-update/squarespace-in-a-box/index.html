Squarespace in a Box

You need a website and you need it now! But you don't know where to get started with it and you don't have a five-figure budget. You're prepared to DIY, but you could use some help.

What is Squarespace in a box?

It's a Squarespace website with all of the parts that you don't know that you need. You work with your designer to figure out what you need where and how to DIY all the parts because you don't have $5,000 to spend on your first website.

What you'll get...

  • Resources on DIYing your brand
  • A custom content plan and guided resources to create that content
  • 2 1-hour consultation calls
  • A fully-loaded Squarespace website that you know how to use

Who is this for?

You're a freelancer now! You're building a client base and now you're ready to turn strangers into clients with your website. You want to work with someone to figure what to put on your website, get it up and running in 30 days, and be able to manage it on your own.
